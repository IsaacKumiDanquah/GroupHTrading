Our Rich History

GROUP H Trading Enterprise was founded in October 2001 with the singular objective of furnishing accessible electronic appliances to the Ghanaian populace. Presently, our esteemed organization proudly boasts a network of 500 branches across the globe.


Our Vision statement

Our ultimate aspiration is to become the preeminent and unwavering purveyor of exceptional electronic devices worldwide.

OUR LOCATION

Our branches have proliferated throughout all 16 regions of Ghana, with our principal location situated in Abuakwa, which is located in the Ashanti region.
